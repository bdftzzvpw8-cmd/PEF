My dearest Paige,

I keep starting this letter and stopping, because there is no clever first line that does justice to what I actually want to say. So I'll say it plainly: meeting you was the best thing to happen to me in a very long time.

From the very first time we talked, you made all the noise go quiet. You're sharp and funny and impossibly easy to be around, and I've caught myself looking forward to the smallest, most ordinary moments simply because they have you in them.

I don't know everything that's ahead of us — but I know I want to find out with you. No rush and no script. Just you and me, figuring out the good parts as we go.

Here's to whatever comes next.

Always yours,

David
